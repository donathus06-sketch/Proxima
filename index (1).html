FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about working with Proxima Solution.

What is an AI video ad and how is it different?

+

An AI video ad uses AI-accelerated production tools alongside expert strategy and editing to create high-quality video creative faster and more affordably than traditional production, without sacrificing quality.

How long does it take to receive my video ads?

+

Most projects are delivered within 2 to 5 business days depending on package and complexity. Growth and Enterprise clients receive priority 48-hour turnaround.

Which platforms do you create ads for?

+

We produce video ads optimized for TikTok, Instagram (Reels & Feed), Facebook, and YouTube (Shorts & in-stream), including the correct dimensions and specs for each.

Do you offer revisions?

+

Yes. Starter plans include 2 rounds of revisions, while Growth and Enterprise plans include unlimited revisions until you're fully satisfied.

Do I need my own footage or assets?

+

Not necessarily. We can produce fully AI-generated creative, or combine your existing brand assets and product footage with AI-enhanced production for a hybrid result.
